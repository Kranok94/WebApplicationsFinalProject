About

Gadget XChange is an event hosted by the Student Council of Engineering in Digital Systems and Robotics and Engineering in Electronical Technologies(SAISD-SAITE). The event consists on selling products (typically electronic products or related). People around Campus can bring their own things that they want to sell and they will be displayed on a stand put by the Student Council. Then, another person can buy that product for the price set by the owner.

For each product that is sold, the Student Council charges a remuneration of 10% of the product's price that is placed by the owner. Additionally, there is $20 pesos charge of rent for every product that is exhibited. The products that are available in the stand can be seen by clicking the "Products" button in the Home Page.

If you have any devices or products that you want to sell, Gadget XChange is the event that will make that possible.
